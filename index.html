What is the LCR calculator?
The LCR calculator is a tool that computes the liquidity coverage ratio.
How to calculate LCR?
To calculate LCR you should use the following formula:
LCR = (cash and cash equivalents + marketable securities) / expected 30-days cash outflows
Why use LCR calculator?
The LCR calculator is used in order to measure the liquidity coverage ratio.
